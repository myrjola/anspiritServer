Company history

Anspirit started on November 2015 in Junction. After that we started developing Q further!
We started developing from QDesktop and Server Side. On January we ordered parts for our first prototype
and started working on Q OS, operating system for QHub. We went to Startup Cup @ Haaga Helia in January.
That was cool and useful experience!
We sent our application to Vertical and now waiting for result. There we will get coaching and possible  150 000 euro of investment. On 2nd of February we got our Microsoft BizSpark subscription. Now we have lot of cool things from Microsoft, like Azure, Microsoft Office and lot of cool stuff.
