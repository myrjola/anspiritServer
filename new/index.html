Q is here to make it easy to communicate with IoT for users and developers in the easiest ways.
it is a cloud based personal AI with cross platform clients.
Q connects every accessible device. Also Q can recognize emotions, gestures, voice and user’s mood.
Q will adopt atmosphere personally for user.

Control over own life is another step to future of IoT

With Q you will be able to access all Internet of Things features! You can control your stuff wherever you are. You are at office but forgot lights on? Or you are not sure did you? You can check it and turn them off or on if needed! You can change lamps color simply by saying what you want or if Q will think what you are tired, it will try to make better atmosphere there where you are! Do you want coffee? Turn oven on when you are away, or just to busy that you can’t do it yourself? Or you are simply lying on your bed and want to play music? Simply say what you want! Q will do it...

What do you want?

Have you ever been in this situation when you are coming home tired and want some coffee or sleep or both? Q can communicate with the user and ask it about its regards. If user want something, Q will do it’s best to make it real! Let’s imagine user is a big fan of Justin Bieber and he will have concert in user’s city soon. Q will tell about this to user and ask him if he want to go! Also Q will check if user’s friends are going as well.

Assistant and friend

Q is just perfect to live in your office, in your bedroom, kitchen, living room! Imagine you are sitting on your sofa and want to know whether outside or you just woke up and you want coffee, just ask Q for coffee and some fresh news! Ok now let's imagine that you have question is your mind… Something like “Who is Taylor Swift?” or “When 2nd World War began?” etc. You can ask whatever you want. Also you can set smart alarms, notification, calendar events… All this will be synced with cloud and no need to care that you will not be reminded!

Open Source

Open Source is our future! Q is Open Source as well! Whoever can develop extensions for Q or make there own version of Q… We have marketplace for users to extend features of their Q and for developers to make some cash or make extension free! Q is fully written on node.js, best programming language ever! If you made your own coffee machine but Q can’t access it, you can it’s support by yourself. The future is now!
